Core Technology

Leveraging state-of-the-art computer vision to map 21 key points of the hand with sub-millimeter precision, tracking every subtle movement without markers or wearables.

🎮

Transforming repetitive therapy into engaging interactive challenges that keep patients motivated for longer, more effective sessions.
